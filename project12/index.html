Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Goldendoodle?

Characterized as a designer dog, this breed was created by crossing a Golden Retriever with a Poodle.

What can you tell me about their traits?

They have superior intelligence which makes them easy to train and they are not usually considered barkers.

What kind of coat do they have?

Their coat can be slightly wavy (closer to their Golden Retriever side) or more curly or wiry (closer to the Poodle coat).

How do I care for their fur?

In order to avoid matting, brushing your Goldendoodle multiple times throughout the week is a good habit to develop.

Should I worry about shedding?

A big reason many people purchase goldendoodles is to have an amazing companion without all of the pesky hair everywhere.
